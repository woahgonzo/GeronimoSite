Who We Are

The Collective

GERONIMO

Geronimo Records is a Las Vegas–based creative collective dedicated to building culture from the inside out.

We produce events across music, fashion, and film — connecting artists, designers, and audiences in a city that deserves its own scene. Not a replica of anywhere else. Ours.

From intimate concerts to citywide showcases, we believe every event is an opportunity to document who Las Vegas is becoming.
